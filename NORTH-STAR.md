# Caffeine Designer -- North Star

**Type:** market  

A desktop tool for visually designing professional app specifications through click-only choices, with portfolio management, live app operations, and an app marketplace for Builders and Launchers.

## Features (as described)

- Click-only spec builder with 10-section professional export
- Builder and Launcher split dashboard on Home
- Projects table with rich detail workspace and 10 tabs
- Dense live app management dashboard with full operational controls
- App Market with creator profiles, pricing advisor, and post-clone guided setup
- 6-step Launcher onboarding flow after cloning (Brand, Content, Features, Updates, Go Live)
- Wired pricing advisor in publish flow with formula-based price suggestions
- Circular Lucide icons throughout with no letter avatars
- All card surfaces pure white with visible borders and shadow elevation
